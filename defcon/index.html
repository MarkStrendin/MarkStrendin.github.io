Where to find DEF CON 29 content

For people who don't want to use Discord to find this stuff. You do not need an account to watch Youtube or Twitch streams.

Links will be updated as I discover them.

Most schedules will be based on PDT (Las Vegas time), so you will need to adjust accordingly. Google "PDT" to see what time it is in that time zone, and compare that to the time wherever you are currently standing.

Official info/schedule page: https://info.defcon.org/

Demo Labs

Demo labs are smaller live sessions that happen in the vendor's Discord channel on the official DEFCON Discord server. Some of these are open source products being demoed, some may be sales pitches, some may just be someone showing off something cool.
